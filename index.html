WORKOUT TIPS & ADVICE:

  • Why is Daily Exercise Important?
    Daily exercise is essential for maintaining overall health. It improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, boosts mood, and helps reduce stress. Engaging in physical activity regularly also lowers the risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes and hypertension.




  • How Much Exercise is Enough?
    Experts recommend at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise daily. This can include activities like brisk walking, cycling, or yoga. For those looking to achieve specific fitness goals, incorporating strength training and high-intensity workouts a few times a week can be beneficial.




  • What is the Best Time to Exercise?
    The best time to exercise varies from person to person. For some, morning workouts provide an energy boost and help kickstart the day. Others may find evening workouts more relaxing after a long day. The key is consistency—choose a time that fits your schedule and feels most comfortable for you.




  • Does Exercise Affect Sleep?
    Absolutely! Regularexercise has been shown to improve sleep quality and duration. It helps regulate your body’s internal clock, reduces stress, and promotes relaxation. However, avoid intense workouts close to bedtime, as they might temporarily energize you and make it harder to fall asleep.
